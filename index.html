Final Takeaway

Not all cereals are as healthy as they seem. By interacting with the charts, users can discover that sugar, fiber, calories, and brand all affect how healthy a cereal looks.

🍬 Higher sugar cereals usually have lower ratings.
🌾 Higher fiber cereals usually look healthier overall.
📊 Interactive charts help users discover patterns themselves.
