Key Takeaways

What the data tells us about breakfast cereals.

🍬

Sugary cereals score lower. Higher sugar content correlates with worse consumer ratings across brands.

🌾

Fiber is the health marker. Cereals with high fiber tend to rate better and have lower sugar.

🏷️

Brand matters less than ingredients. Every manufacturer has both high- and low-sugar options.
